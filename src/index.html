It's a Jellyfish. Y'know that spineless thingy.

Visible, invisible,

A fluctuating charm,

An amber-colored amethyst

Inhabits it; your arm

Approaches, and

It opens and

It closes;

You have meant

To catch it,

And it shrivels;

You abandon

Your intent—

It opens, and it

Closes and you

Reach for it—

The blue

Surrounding it

Grows cloudy, and

It floats away

From you.

Poem by Marianne Moore Published in Poem-a-Day on August 30, 2015, by the Academy of American Poets
